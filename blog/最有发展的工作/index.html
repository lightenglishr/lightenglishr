寻找新的职业？这里有 30 个高薪、快速增长的工作，这些工作将在未来十年蓬勃发展

Looking for a new career? Here are 30 high-paying, fast growing jobs that are set to boom over the next decade

Several high-paying managerial, tech, and medical jobs are projected to see strong growth between 2020 and 2030. 预计 2020 年至 2030 年间，一些高薪的管理、技术和医疗工作将出现强劲增长。

Millions of Americans are looking for new careers amid a pandemic reshuffle, and these high-paying, fast-growing jobs could be a good fit. We used 2020–2030 employment projections and 2020 wage data to find high-paying jobs that are expected to grow. Managerial, tech, and medical jobs like registered nurses made up some of the top 30 occupations. Here are 30 high-paying jobs that are set to boom over the next decade: 在大流行的重新洗牌中，数百万美国人正在寻找新的职业，而这些高薪、快速增长的工作可能非常适合。 我们使用 2020-2030 年的就业预测和 2020 年的工资数据来寻找预计会增长的高薪工作。管理、技术和医疗工作（如注册护士）构成了前 30 名职业中的一些。 以下是 30 个将在未来十年蓬勃发展的高薪工作：

  1. Electricians 电工

  2. All other computer occupations 所有其他计算机职业

  3. Ophthalmologists (except pediatric) and all other physicians眼科医生（儿科除外）和所有其他医生

  4. Computer systems analysts 计算机系统分析师

  5. Marketing managers 营销经理

  6. Logisticians 物流师

  7. Human resources specialists 人力资源专家

  8. Physical therapists 物理治疗师

  9. Physician assistants 医师助理

  10. Industrial machinery mechanics 工业机械力学

  11. Information security analysts 信息安全分析师

  12. All other personal service managers; entertainment and recreation managers (except gambling); and all other managers 所有其他个人服务经理；娱乐和休闲经理（赌博除外）；和所有其他经理

  13. Secondary school teachers (except special and career/technical education) 中学教师（特殊和职业/技术教育除外）

  14. Construction managers 施工经理

  15. Sales representatives of services (except advertising, insurance, financial services, and travel) 服务销售代表（广告、保险、金融服务和旅游除外）

  16. Heavy and tractor-trailer truck drivers 重型和拖拉机拖车司机

  17. Postsecondary health specialties teachers 大专卫生专业教师

  18. Elementary school teachers (except special education) 小学教师（特殊教育除外）

  19. Accountants and auditors 会计师和审计师

  20. All other project management specialists and business operations specialists 所有其他项目管理专家和业务运营专家

  21. Computer and information systems managers 计算机和信息系统经理

  22. Lawyers 律师

  23. Market research analysts and marketing specialists 市场研究分析师和营销专家

  24. Management analysts 管理分析师

  25. Nurse practitioners 执业护士

  26. Medical and health services managers 医疗卫生服务经理

  27. Financial managers 金融经理

  28. Registered nurses 注册护士

  29. General and operations managers 总经理和运营经理

  30. Software developers and software quality assurance analysts and testers 软件开发人员和软件质量保证分析师和测试人员
